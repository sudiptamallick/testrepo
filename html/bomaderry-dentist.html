Payment and billing services

Establishing a trusting relationship with you is important to us at Bomaderry Dental Surgery.

  • We bulk bill Medicare directly for Medicare Child Dental Benefits Schedule and Department of Veterans Affairs patients.
  • Bomaderry Dental Surgery is a network provider with Medibank Private and HCF, so you can enjoy greater value with your cover.
  • We also have HICAPS so you can claim your health fund benefits on the spot.. We have EFTPOS facilities for credit and debit cards.
  • Bomaderry Dental Surgery is pleased to offer a patient payment plan through Denticare. (available to approved applicants, conditions apply).
