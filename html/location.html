Location of Bomaderry Dental Surgery

Our address

1/6 Bunberra Street (entrance in Coomea Street), Bomaderry NSW 2541.

Our dental surgery is conveniently located in the heart of Bomaderry and easily accessible to all areas of the Shoalhaven and South Coast. We are within walking distance of Bomaderry railway and bus station.

Access

We are located on the first floor of the building, however we do have a stair lift for patients who have difficulty climbing stairs. Access to the surgery is not suitable for wheelchairs.

Locations we serve

We serve patients who live throughout the Shoalhaven area and further afield on the South Coast. In particular our patients often come from the following locations:

  • Berry
  • Bomaderry
  • Kangaroo Valley
  • Nowra

Our Services

We offer a full range of dental treatments, to patients in the north Shoalhaven area, including:

  • Check ups & x-rays
  • Teeth whitening
  • Dental implants
  • Wisdom teeth extraction and oral surgery
  • Orthodontics
