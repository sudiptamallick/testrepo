Dr Brett Eaglen(dentist)
and our team

  • Dr Brett Eaglen

    Dr Brett Eaglen is the principal dentist at Bomaderry Dental Surgery and has built an excellent reputation throughout the Shoalhaven and South Coast as a leader in dental medicine.He is also an oral surgeon and orthodontist.

    Dr Eaglen's reputation has been built upon his education, experience and desire to always remain at the forefront of innovation in dentistry. He began his training at Queensland University, graduating in 1982. Since then he has trained extensively in advanced dentistry topics, making him an expert in general dentistry. His particular areas of interest include implants and sailing!

  • Ailssia Hughes

    Ailssia Hughes is an Oral Health Therapist at Bomaderry Dental Surgery.

    Ailssia graduated from the University of Newcastle in 2008 after completing a Bachelor of Oral Health and a Post Graduate Diploma in Dental Therapy in 2012.

    Ailssia has been a valuable addition to the practice enabling us to provide a full range of dental treatment. Her specialty is children's dentistry and periodontics.
