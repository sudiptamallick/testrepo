Just 20 minutes from Kangaroo Valley

Bomaderry Dental Surgery is ideally located for residents of Kangaroo Valley. There is no need to cross the bridge as we are conveniently located near the railway station in Bomaderry with plenty of parking available. We are located next to Your Mouth Matters denture clinic and work closely with this business should a patient require dentures.

Dental treatments

We have two dentists and a dental therapist and provide a full range of dental treatments from check ups, fillings through to oral surgery and implants.

Billing and payments

Bomaderry Dental Surgery is able to process private health insurance claims on the spot through the HICAPS system. We are also a Medibank First Choice Provider and registered for the HCF More for Teeth programme which ensures members of better value for their membership. The surgery also has EFTPOS facilities for those paying by debit or credit card.

We are pleased to offer a patient payment plan through Denticare (available to approved applicants, conditions apply).

Our Services

We offer a full range of dental treatments, to patients in the north Shoalhaven area, including:

  • Check ups & x-rays
  • Teeth Whitening
  • Dental implants
  • Wisdom teeth extraction and oral surgery
  • Orthodontics
