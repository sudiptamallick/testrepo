Convenient location for Nowra residents

Bomaderry Dental Surgery is the ideal choice for residents of Nowra to visit for all their dental treatment. Our friendly competent staff will ensure that each visit is stress-free. Our extended opening hours from 8am until late on Monday to Friday make it easy for appointments to be made before/after school and work. There is also plenty of parking available by the surgery.

No need to travel to Sydney or Canberra

There is no need for Nowra residents to travel to Sydney or Canberra for oral surgery or dental implants. Dr Brett Eaglen is very experienced in both these areas of dental treatment and regularly attends courses to further increase his knowledge base. Read more about our services.

Easy payments and billing

Bomaderry Dental Surgery welcomes children who are eligible for the new Government Childrens Dental Benefits Schedule and will bulk bill for this treatment. We give all other children under 16 years of age 30% discount for most treatment.

Bomaderry Dental Surgery is pleased to offer a patient payment plan through Denticare (available to approved applicants, conditions apply).
