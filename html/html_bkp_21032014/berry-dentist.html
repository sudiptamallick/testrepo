Berry dentist –
Bomaderry Dental Surgery

Just 15 minutes from Berry

Bomaderry Dental Surgery is a short 15 minute drive from the township of Berry making it the ideal location for residents of Berry to visit for all aspects of dental treatment. We are also within a short walk of Bomaderry railway station, making it an easy journey by train from Berry.

Patients can organise appointments in conjunction with a shopping trip to nearby Nowra, and our extended opening hours make it possible for students to visit before or after school.

Dental services

Dr Eaglen and his team offer a complete range of dentistry services to Berry patients, from regular check-ups through to dental implants, one of Dr Eaglen’s particular specialities. Read more about our services.

Payment and billing services

For patients' convenience we offer the following services:

  • Bulk billing accounts for children eligible for Child Dental Benefits Schedule
  • Veteran Affairs
  • HICAPS & EFTPOS facilities
  • 30% discount for children under the age of 16 years for most treatment

Bomaderry Dental Surgery is pleased to offer a patient payment plan through Denticare (available to approved applicants, conditions apply).

Our Services

We offer a full range of dental treatments, to patients in the north Shoalhaven area, including:

  • Check ups & x-rays
  • Teeth Whitening
  • Dental implants
  • Wisdom teeth extraction and oral surgery
  • Orthodontics
